# 🛠️ Chiromo Forge

Welcome to **Chiromo Forge**, the development hub for the [Chiromo Platform](https://chiromo.tech/). We are on a mission to democratize innovation by providing a space where builders can access, create, and refine projects through hackathons.

## 🚀 Our Mission
To empower the next generation of innovators by connecting builders, organizers, and creators in a vibrant ecosystem of collaboration.

## 🏗️ What We’re Building
*   **Chiromo Platform:** The complete platform for hackathon organizers and participants.
*   **Open Source Tools:** Utilities and templates to help hackers build better projects, faster.
*   **Community Projects:** Solutions created during our events that aim for real-world impact.
